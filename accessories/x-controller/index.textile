  <h3>Attach Power Supply to Chassis</h3>

  <img src="P8200936Editcopy.jpg">

  <p>Using the included hardware, attach the power supply through the mounting holes on the underside of the chassis.
  </p>

  <h3>Attach Stops</h3>

  <img src="P8200944Editcopy.jpg">

  <p>Install the screws in the threaded holes on the side of the chassis.  These screws locate the back of the controller board and make the face of the board flush with the chassis.</p>

  <h3>Wire Controller Board</h3>

  <img src="P8200993Editcopy.jpg">

  <p>Attach the cables to the PCB Interface board.  There is only one way to attach the cables, so if they don't attach well, double check them and try again.  Make sure to tuck the e-stop cable along the edge of the enclosed power supply to prevent any sort of snagging or pinching.</p>
